“Spiritual Spots in Janakpurdham”

There are a number of spiritual locations in Janakpurdham for tourists. Goddess Sita is honoured in the hallowed Janaki Mandir, while Lord Rama is honoured in the Ram Mandir. The Vivah Mandap also designates the location of Rama and Sita's celestial wedding. The Hanuman Mandir is a temple devoted to Lord Hanuman that draws pilgrims and enhances the spiritual atmosphere of the city.
