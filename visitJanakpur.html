Top Places to visit in janakpurdham

Visits to the magnificent Janaki Mandir, which is devoted to Goddess Sita, and the Ram Mandir, which honours Lord Rama, are among the top attractions in Janakpurdham. Of utmost importance is the holy Mandap, the location of their celestial union. See Lord Shiva's bow-related Dhanusha Mandir and the sacred pond Ganga Sagar, which is encircled by temples. Look for traditional Maithili crafts and souvenirs at the lively local markets as well.
